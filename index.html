What is Romiromi

A traditional Māori hands-on healing practice

Romiromi works with the whole person — physical body, mind, wairua and whakapapa. Treatments are provided fully clothed and involve a full body alignment from head to toe, front and back, using acupressure, bodywork and traditional Māori philosophy.

My style of Romiromi is guided by gentle, nurturing feminine energies. It is trauma-informed, deeply respectful, and creates a safe space for your nervous system to let go of survival mode and return to deep rest.

Using the physical body as the gateway, Romiromi reaches beyond muscles and joints into the deeper layers of your being. As tension begins to soften, many people notice emotional burdens becoming lighter, breathing becoming easier, thoughts becoming quieter, and their body moving with greater freedom.

"The body keeps the score." Dr. Bessel van der Kolk

Our bodies often carry the stories of stress, grief, trauma and emotional pain long after the events themselves have passed. Romiromi gently supports the release of these stored patterns through the nervous system, helping to reduce physical tension while restoring a greater sense of balance, safety and connection within yourself.

Rooted in Rongoā Māori

Romiromi is part of the wider healing tradition of Rongoā Māori — a holistic system of healing that recognises the interconnectedness of tinana (body), hinengaro (mind), wairua (spirit), whakapapa and our relationship with te taiao (the natural world).

Research by Dr Glenis Mark describes Rongoā Māori as an intricate and dynamic system of healing relationships that supports the restoration of wellbeing.

After treatment, many describe feeling…

  • Lighter, calmer, more grounded and more present
  • Pain eases and movement improves
  • Sleep becomes deeper
  • Emotions feel more settled
  • The body remembers what it feels like to truly rest
The Practice

I don't simply treat the body —
I work through four interconnected dimensions

With Feminine Energies Romiromi, healing moves through a living lattice of body, mind, spirit and lineage.

Tinana

The Physical Body

Relieving pain, restoring movement and easing physical tension.

Hinengaro

The Mind

Calming the nervous system, reducing mental overload and emotional stress.

Wairua

The Spirit

Reconnecting people with themselves, their inner spark and sense of purpose.

Whakapapa

The Generations

Acknowledging that healing can ripple through generations by restoring what has always belonged within us.

Operating within this living lattice is incredibly powerful; it is firmly grounded in the ancient wisdom of Te Ao Māori while also resonating with current neuroscience regarding trauma and nervous system regulation.

At a deeper level, Romiromi acknowledges the connection between wairua and whakapapa. It offers a space to gently clear inherited burdens, strengthen your connection to your ancestors, and restore the internal richness, resilience and wellbeing that can be carried forward to future generations — "Generational Wealth."
